# Products & Services
> Your complete catalog — organized, priced, and ready to sell.

## The Problem
Products scattered across spreadsheets, prices that don't match between sales and purchasing, and no way to track what's actually in stock at each warehouse. When your catalog is disconnected from your invoices and POS, every transaction becomes a manual data entry exercise — and errors multiply.

## How It Works
Bojeri's Products & Services module is the foundation that powers your invoices, proposals, POS transactions, and purchase orders. Create items with detailed profiles: name, SKU, description, sale price, purchase price, unit of measurement, and type (product or service). Assign items to **categories** for organized browsing and apply **tax rates** that automatically carry through to invoices and proposals. Set up **units** (pieces, kg, liters, hours) for accurate quantity tracking. Every item tracks **warehouse stock levels** — when you create an invoice or transfer, the system checks real-time quantities from specific warehouses. Products can be marked active or inactive, and the System Setup section lets you configure categories, taxes, and units that power the entire catalog.

## Key Benefits
- **Centralize your catalog** with items that flow into invoices, proposals, POS, and purchase orders automatically
- **Set dual pricing** — separate sale and purchase prices for accurate margin tracking
- **Track warehouse stock** per location with real-time quantity checks during transactions
- **Organize with categories** to keep products and services structured and searchable
- **Apply tax rates** at the item level that automatically carry through to every transaction
- **Define measurement units** — pieces, kg, hours, or any custom unit your business needs
- **Toggle item status** with active/inactive flags to manage seasonal or discontinued products
- **Search by SKU** for fast lookup during invoicing and point-of-sale transactions

## Who This Is For
**Inventory Managers** — Maintain accurate product catalogs with stock levels across warehouses.
**Sales Teams** — Pull products into invoices and proposals with prices and taxes pre-loaded.
**Finance Teams** — Ensure consistent pricing and tax rates across all transactions.

## Real-World Example
A building materials company manages 500+ products across three warehouses. They set up categories — "Plumbing," "Electrical," "Hardware" — and add each product with SKU, sale price, purchase price, and applicable taxes (10% VAT, 2% local levy). When the sales team creates an invoice, they pick "Downtown Warehouse" and see only products with available stock. The system auto-fills the sale price and taxes. When procurement creates a purchase order, the purchase price is used instead. One catalog, consistent data, zero re-entry.

## Frequently Asked Questions
**Q: Do product prices and taxes sync to invoices automatically?**
A: Yes. When you add a product to a Sales Invoice, Purchase Invoice, or Proposal, the system pulls the appropriate price and any assigned taxes automatically. You can still override them per transaction.

**Q: Can I track the same product across multiple warehouses?**
A: Absolutely. Each product has per-warehouse stock quantities. When creating transactions, you choose the warehouse and see only items with available stock at that location.
