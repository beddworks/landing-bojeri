# Dashboard
> Your entire business at a glance — one screen, zero guesswork.

## The Problem
Running a business means juggling numbers from a dozen different places — sales spreadsheets, HR emails, project trackers, CRM notes. By the time you've pieced together the full picture, the moment to act has already passed. You need real-time visibility, not yesterday's data pulled from five different tabs.

## How It Works
The moment you log in, your Dashboard loads a live snapshot of what matters most. Bojeri offers five purpose-built dashboard views — **Account**, **Project**, **HRM**, **POS**, and **CRM** — each tailored to a specific area of your business. The Super Admin Dashboard tracks total order payments, plan subscriptions, company registrations, and monthly revenue charts automatically. Company dashboards dynamically display module-specific insights based on which modules your team has activated. Each dashboard pulls real-time data from your actual transactions, tasks, attendance records, and sales pipeline — no manual refreshes, no stale numbers.

## Key Benefits
- **See monthly revenue trends** with auto-generated charts tracking orders and payments across all twelve months
- **Monitor plan adoption** with live counts of total plans, companies, and active subscriptions
- **Switch dashboard views** between Account, Project, HRM, POS, and CRM to focus on what matters right now
- **Get role-specific insights** — Super Admins see platform-wide metrics; company users see their own business data
- **Load instantly** with cached settings and optimized queries that keep your dashboard responsive
- **Access from anywhere** with a fully responsive, Inertia.js-powered interface that works on desktop and mobile

## Who This Is For
**Business Owners** — Get a morning briefing on revenue, orders, and company growth without opening a single spreadsheet.
**Project Managers** — Switch to the Project Dashboard to see task progress, milestones, and team activity at a glance.
**HR Managers** — Monitor attendance, leave requests, and payroll status without chasing down reports.

## Real-World Example
Lina runs a growing consultancy with 35 employees. Every Monday morning, she opens Bojeri and lands on her Account Dashboard — total revenue this month is up 12%, and she can see exactly which months drove growth in the annual chart. She clicks over to the HRM Dashboard to check that all attendance records are submitted before payroll runs Wednesday. Then she switches to the CRM Dashboard to see how many deals moved through the pipeline last week. Three dashboards, five minutes, and she has a complete picture of her business without a single spreadsheet or follow-up email.

## Frequently Asked Questions
**Q: Can I customize which dashboard I see first?**
A: Yes. The system automatically detects your active modules and redirects you to the most relevant dashboard for your role and permissions.

**Q: Does the dashboard update in real time?**
A: Dashboard data is pulled fresh on each page load from your actual transactions, attendance records, and sales data — so you always see the latest numbers.
