## Comparison 1: Bojeri vs. Spreadsheets + Email

### Total Cost

| Factor | Spreadsheets + Email | Bojeri |
|--------|---------------------|-------|
| Software cost | "Free" (but hidden in time) | Transparent monthly/annual plans |
| Setup time | Ongoing — you build the system forever | One-time setup in hours, not weeks |
| Error correction | Manual — find and fix typos in formulas | Validated inputs & auto-calculations |
| Training cost | Everyone invents their own system | Consistent interface, one system to learn |

**Reality check:** Spreadsheets aren't free when you factor in the 10–15 hours per week your team spends maintaining them, reconciling versions, and fixing formula errors.

| Factor | Spreadsheets + Email | Bojeri |
|--------|---------------------|-------|

| Product catalog | Separate price lists per department | One catalog with sale/purchase prices, taxes, warehouse stock |
| Financial data | Separate sheets for revenue, expenses, bank | Unified Chart of Accounts with auto-linked transactions |

### Time to Insight

| Factor | Spreadsheets + Email | Bojeri |
|--------|---------------------|-------|

| Outstanding invoices | Manual filtering and counting | One glance at the Sales Invoice list, filtered by status |
| Employee attendance | Paper or manual logs, compiled weekly | Real-time clock in/out with auto overtime calculation |
| Pipeline health | Memory or manual CRM sheets | CRM Reports show lead-to-deal conversion instantly |

### Scalability

| Factor | Spreadsheets + Email | Bojeri |
|--------|---------------------|-------|
| Adding team members | Share drive access, create new sheets | Add user, assign role — done in 30 seconds |
| New locations | Duplicate and customize sheets | Create a new warehouse, assign products, start |

### Audit Trail

| Factor | Spreadsheets + Email | Bojeri |
|--------|---------------------|-------|
