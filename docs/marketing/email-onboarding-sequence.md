# Email Onboarding Sequence

Five emails to guide new users from signup to productive use. Each email references real platform features.

---

## Email 1 — Day 0: Welcome + First Action

**Subject:** Welcome to Bojeri — let's get your workspace ready
**Preview text:** Set up your company profile in 3 minutes and you're in business.

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

Welcome to Bojeri! You've just taken the first step toward running your entire business from one platform.

Right now, the most important thing is setting up your workspace. Head to **Settings** and add your company name, logo, and currency format. This takes about 3 minutes and ensures every invoice, proposal, and report you create carries your brand.

While you're there, set your **date format** and **default language** — these preferences flow through every screen in the platform.

Once your workspace looks right, try adding your first team member under **User Management → Users**. Assign them a role (we've pre-configured Staff, Client, and Vendor roles for you), and they'll get instant access with the right permissions.

Your dashboard is already waiting with live metrics — the more you use Bojeri, the more useful it becomes.

**CTA:** Set Up Your Workspace →

## Email 2 — Day 2: First Invoice or Proposal

**Subject:** Your first invoice is just 3 clicks away
**Preview text:** Create a Sales Invoice or Proposal and see the magic of auto-calculations.

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

Now that your workspace is set up, let's create your first transaction.

**Option A: Create a Sales Invoice**
Go to **Sales Invoice → Sales Invoice**, click "Create," select a customer, and add your products. Watch the system auto-calculate subtotals, taxes, and discounts as you add line items. Save as Draft, review, then Post to lock it.

**Option B: Send a Proposal**
Go to **Proposal**, build a quote for a client with products from your catalog, and click "Send." When your client accepts, convert it to an invoice with one click — all line items, taxes, and notes carry over.

Either way, you'll see the power of connected data immediately. Your products, prices, taxes, and customer details are shared across the entire platform — enter them once, use them everywhere.

**Pro tip:** Add your products first under **Product & Service → Items** with sale prices and tax rates. That way, every invoice and proposal pulls the right numbers automatically.

**CTA:** Create Your First Invoice →

## Email 3 — Day 4: Explore HRM or CRM

**Subject:** Two tools your team will thank you for
**Preview text:** Track attendance automatically or manage your sales pipeline — both built right in.

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

You've set up your workspace and created your first transaction. Now let's unlock two tools that transform how your team works.

**For managing people: HRM**
Add employees, configure shifts with start/end times and break periods, and let your team clock in and out. The system calculates total hours, overtime, and attendance status (Present, Half Day, Absent) automatically — even for night shifts. At month end, run payroll with all the data already calculated.

**For managing customers: CRM**
Create leads from your sales pipeline, move them through custom stages (Draft → Sent → Open → Revised → Accepted), and convert qualified leads to Deals. Track deal progress through pipeline stages — Initial Contact, Qualification, Meeting, Proposal, Close. Reports show you exactly where your pipeline stands.

Both modules share your user database and permissions, so there's no separate setup or data re-entry. Just activate the module on your plan and you're ready.

**CTA:** Explore HRM → | Explore CRM →

## Email 4 — Day 7: "Did you know?" — Hidden Gem

**Subject:** 3 things about Bojeri you probably haven't found yet
**Preview text:** Built-in messenger, file library, and multi-language support — all included.

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

You've been using Bojeri for a week now — here are three features hiding in plain sight that our most productive teams love:

**1. Built-In Messenger**
You don't need Slack or Teams. Open **Messenger** and you'll see your entire team listed with online/offline status. Send messages, share files, pin important conversations, and mark favorites — all powered by real-time delivery so messages appear instantly.

**2. Media Library**
Every file your team works with — invoices, project documents, design assets — lives in one place. Go to **Media Library**, create directories to organize by project or department, and batch-upload files. Supports AWS S3 and Wasabi if you need cloud storage.

**3. Full Multi-Language Support**
Under **Settings → Languages**, you can create any language, edit every translation string, and even manage package-specific translations. Perfect if your team operates across multiple countries.

These aren't add-ons. They're built into every plan, ready to use right now.

**CTA:** Open Messenger → | Browse Media Library →
