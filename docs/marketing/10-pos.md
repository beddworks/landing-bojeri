# POS
> Sell in person, sync to your books — every transaction, every barcode.

## The Problem
Retail businesses that track in-store sales separately from their accounting and inventory systems end up with two versions of the truth. Manual barcode printing, disconnected registers, and no real-time stock updates mean discrepancies pile up until month-end reconciliation becomes a multi-day headache. You need a point-of-sale system that's part of your ERP, not another standalone tool to manage.

## How It Works
Bojeri's POS module turns your browser into a full point-of-sale terminal. Open **Add POS** to start a new transaction — search or scan products from your catalog, add items to the cart with quantities and prices pulled from your Product & Service catalog. The system calculates totals, applies taxes, and processes the sale. Each transaction creates a **POS Order** with a complete record of items sold, quantities, prices, and payment details. Access **POS Orders** to review, search, and filter past transactions. **Print Barcode** generates barcodes for your products, ready to print and attach to physical inventory. POS **Reports** give you daily, weekly, and monthly sales analytics to track performance. Because POS shares your product catalog and warehouse stock, every sale immediately affects your inventory levels.

## Key Benefits
- **Sell from your browser** — no special hardware required, works on any device with a screen
- **Pull products from your catalog** with prices, taxes, and stock levels already configured
- **Track every transaction** with complete order records including items, quantities, and payments
- **Print barcodes** for physical products directly from the system
- **Generate sales reports** with daily, weekly, and monthly breakdowns
- **Sync with inventory** — every POS sale updates your warehouse stock levels automatically
- **Maintain one source of truth** — POS transactions feed into your accounting and financial reports
- **Control access** with permissions for creating sales, viewing orders, and printing barcodes

## Who This Is For
**Retail Store Managers** — Run in-store sales with real-time inventory sync and barcode printing.
**Franchise Owners** — Track POS transactions across multiple locations from one dashboard.
**Cashiers** — Process sales quickly with a clean, intuitive interface.

## Real-World Example
A boutique clothing store with two locations uses Bojeri POS. Each morning, the cashier opens the POS terminal in their browser. When a customer brings three items to the counter, the cashier searches by product name or scans the printed barcode — items appear with prices and taxes pre-loaded. One click completes the sale, generates an order record, and updates stock at that warehouse. The store manager opens POS Reports to see that the "Downtown" location sold $3,400 today vs. $2,800 at "Mall Location." Meanwhile, new inventory arrives and the manager prints barcodes for 50 new items — stick-on labels ready for the racks.

## Frequently Asked Questions
**Q: Do I need special hardware for the POS?**
A: No. Bojeri POS runs in your web browser on any device — desktop, laptop, or tablet. If you want barcode scanning, any USB or Bluetooth scanner that simulates keyboard input will work.

**Q: Does a POS sale automatically update my inventory?**
A: Yes. Because POS shares your product catalog and warehouse system, every sale deducts from the corresponding warehouse stock in real time.
