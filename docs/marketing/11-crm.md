# CRM
> From first contact to closed deal — track every relationship that matters.

## The Problem
Leads arrive from everywhere — emails, calls, referrals, web forms — and without a system, they slip through the cracks. Your sales team can't see where each prospect stands, which deals are stalling, or which sources generate the most revenue. Juggling a separate CRM tool means data never syncs with your invoices, proposals, and project records.

## How It Works
Bojeri's CRM module tracks your entire sales pipeline from lead capture to deal closure. **Leads** represent initial contacts — create them with names, email, phone, source, and assign team members. Leads live in customizable **stages** (Draft, Sent, Open, Revised, Declined, Accepted) that you can configure to match your sales process. When a lead qualifies, convert it into a **Deal**. Deals have their own stage pipeline (Initial Contact, Qualification, Meeting, Proposal, Close) with assigned team members and clients. Organize everything through **Pipelines** — the default "Sales" pipeline comes pre-configured, but you can create more. Add **Sources** to track where leads originate. Assign multiple **team members** to leads and deals via dedicated relationship tables. **Reports** show lead and deal analytics to measure pipeline health. Clients associated with deals can view their deals through the client portal.

## Key Benefits
- **Track leads with full context** — name, email, phone, source, and assigned team members
- **Move leads through custom stages** — Draft, Sent, Open, Revised, Declined, Accepted (fully configurable)
- **Convert leads to deals** when they qualify, preserving all context and relationships
- **Manage deals through pipeline stages** — Initial Contact, Qualification, Meeting, Proposal, Close
- **Assign multiple team members** to leads and deals for collaborative selling
- **Generate lead and deal reports** to measure conversion rates and pipeline velocity
- **Track deal tasks** with create, edit, and delete permissions for accountability
- **Connect clients to deals** so they can view progress through their portal
- **Configure pipelines and sources** to match your organization's unique sales process
- **Sync with your ERP** — deals connect to proposals, invoices, and project records

## Who This Is For
**Sales Managers** — Monitor pipeline health, track team performance, and forecast revenue.
**Sales Representatives** — Manage leads and deals from first contact to close in one workspace.
**Clients** — View deals they're involved in and track progress through the client portal.

## Real-World Example
A B2B software company generates leads from three sources: website forms, LinkedIn outreach, and industry conferences. The marketing team creates leads in Bojeri CRM with the appropriate source tag. Each lead starts at "Draft" and moves to "Sent" when the sales rep makes first contact. Qualified leads are converted to Deals and progress through "Initial Contact → Qualification → Meeting → Proposal → Close." The sales manager opens CRM Reports every Friday to see: 23 leads this month, 14 moved to Deal stage, 8 are in Proposal, and 3 closed this week. She spots that "Conference" leads convert at 2x the rate of LinkedIn — informing next quarter's budget allocation. When a deal closes, the rep creates a Proposal in Bojeri, sends it, gets acceptance, and converts it to an invoice — all without leaving the platform.

## Frequently Asked Questions
**Q: Can I customize the lead and deal stages?**
A: Yes. Both lead stages and deal stages are fully customizable. The stages come pre-configured with sensible defaults, but you can rename, add, or remove stages through System Setup.

**Q: Does the CRM connect to invoicing and proposals?**
A: Yes. Because CRM is part of the same ERP platform, deals connect naturally to proposals and invoices. When a deal closes, you can create a proposal or invoice directly from the same workspace.
